Machines manufactured specifically for markets outside the United States, often with modifications to comply with local regulations or adapt to regional preferences. Export models might feature different coin mechanisms calibrated for local currency, language changes on the backglass or display, or gameplay adjustments to satisfy jurisdictions with stricter rules about [[gameplayfeature:replay]]s and free games. Some export editions are visually identical to their domestic counterparts; others carry distinct artwork, cabinet colors, or feature sets that make them recognizably different machines.
