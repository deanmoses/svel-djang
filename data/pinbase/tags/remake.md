name: Remake
display_order: 4
---

Machines that are newly manufactured recreations of an earlier title, typically reproducing the original playfield design with updated electronics, improved components, or enhanced features. Remakes are distinguished from restored originals by the fact that they are new production runs, not refurbished vintage machines. [[manufacturer:chicago-gaming]]'s remakes of *[[title:medieval-madness]]* and *[[title:attack-from-mars]]* are prominent examples — new machines built from new parts, faithful to the original designs but manufactured decades after the originals left the factory.
