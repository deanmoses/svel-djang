[[manufacturer:game-plan]]'s first solid-state pinball MPU, used from approximately 1978 to 1980, based on the Motorola 6800 CPU. [[manufacturer:game-plan]] was founded by former [[manufacturer:genco]]/[[manufacturer:chicago-coin]] personnel. Early titles include [[title:sharpshooter]] (1979) and [[title:old-coney-island]] (1979).
