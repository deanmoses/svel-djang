[[manufacturer:capcom]]'s pinball MPU, used during their brief entry into pinball manufacturing from 1995 to 1996. Based on a Motorola 68000 CPU, it featured a dot-matrix display and stereo sound. Notable games include [[title:pinball-magic]] (1995) and [[title:big-bang-bar]] (1996). [[manufacturer:capcom]] exited pinball after producing only a handful of titles.
