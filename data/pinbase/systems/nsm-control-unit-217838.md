The pinball control unit used by NSM (NSM-Löwen), a German manufacturer better known for jukeboxes, during their brief foray into pinball in the mid-1980s. NSM produced only a small number of pinball titles, including [[title:hot-fire-birds]] (1985) and [[title:cosmic-flash]] (1985).
