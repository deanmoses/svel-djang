The proprietary control system used by [[manufacturer:spooky]], a boutique American pinball manufacturer founded in 2011. The PinHeck board powers titles including [[title:americas-most-haunted]] (2014), [[title:rob-zombie]] (2016), [[title:alice-cooper]] (2019), and [[title:rick-and-morty]] (2020). The system uses modern embedded hardware designed for cost-effective small-run production.
