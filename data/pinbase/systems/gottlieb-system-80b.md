A further evolution of the System 80 line, used from approximately 1985 to 1989, still 6502-based but with enhanced sound hardware featuring a Yamaha YM2151 synthesis chip and DAC for digitized speech. Notable games include [[title:monte-carlo-gottlieb]] (1987) and [[title:bone-busters-inc]] (1989).
