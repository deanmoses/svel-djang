A [[manufacturer:sega]] Pinball hardware board from the mid-1990s, part of the evolutionary line from [[manufacturer:data-east]]'s Version 3 platform. It featured a 128x32 DMD and enhanced sound capabilities. Titles on this or adjacent revisions include [[title:apollo-13]] (1995) and [[title:goldeneye]] (1996).
