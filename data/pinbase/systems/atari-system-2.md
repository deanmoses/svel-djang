[[manufacturer:atari]]'s second-generation pinball platform, used from approximately 1977 to 1979, featuring a Motorola 6800 CPU. Games on this platform include [[title:superman]] (1979) and [[title:hercules]] (1979), the latter being the largest pinball machine ever mass-produced. The system introduced speech capability on some later titles.
