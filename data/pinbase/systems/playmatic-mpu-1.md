[[manufacturer:playmatic]]'s first solid-state pinball MPU, used by the Spanish manufacturer [[manufacturer:playmatic]] (Automáticos Montecarlo) beginning in the late 1970s. Based on a CDP 1802 COSMAC processor, an unusual choice that set [[manufacturer:playmatic]] apart from American manufacturers. Early titles include [[title:space-gambler]] (1978).
