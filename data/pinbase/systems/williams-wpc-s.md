WPC-Security, introduced in 1994, added an ASIC security chip to combat ROM piracy and bootleg copies. It retained the DCS sound system. Notable games include [[title:the-flintstones]] (1994), [[title:johnny-mnemonic]] (1995), [[title:jack-bot]] (1995), and [[title:no-fear]] (1995).
