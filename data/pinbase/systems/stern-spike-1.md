[[manufacturer:stern-pinball]]'s SPIKE hardware platform, introduced in 2014 with [[title:wrestlemania]], replacing the SAM system. It uses a distributed node-based architecture where small processor boards throughout the cabinet and playfield connect via a serial bus to a central Linux-based CPU board. Early SPIKE games used a 128x32 DMD; titles include [[title:game-of-thrones]] (2015) and [[title:ghostbusters]] (2016).
