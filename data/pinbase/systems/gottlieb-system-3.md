[[manufacturer:gottlieb]]'s final pinball hardware platform, used from 1989 to 1996, featuring a 65C02 CPU and a 128x32 dot-matrix display. Notable games include [[title:surf-n-safari]] (1991), [[title:cue-ball-wizard]] (1992), and [[title:stargate]] (1995). Premier Technology ([[manufacturer:gottlieb]]'s parent) exited pinball in 1996.
