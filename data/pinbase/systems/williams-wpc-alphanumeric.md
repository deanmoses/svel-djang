The first generation of [[manufacturer:williams]]' WPC ([[manufacturer:williams]] Pinball Controller) platform, introduced in 1990, using a Motorola 6809 CPU. This initial version retained alphanumeric segmented displays. [[title:funhouse]] (1990) and [[title:harley-davidson-bally]] (1991) are notable titles. It established the WPC architecture that would dominate the 1990s.
