The final revision of the System 11 line, used from approximately 1989 to 1990, bridging the gap to the WPC era. Notable games include [[title:riverboat-gambler]] (1990) and [[title:rollergames]] (1990). By this point [[manufacturer:williams]] was developing the WPC platform that would define the 1990s.
