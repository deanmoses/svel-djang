An incremental update to [[manufacturer:gottlieb]]'s System 80, introduced around 1983, retaining the 6502 CPU but adding alphanumeric displays for more expressive scoring and messaging. Games include [[title:rack-em-up]] (1983) and other mid-1980s [[manufacturer:gottlieb]]/Mylstar titles.
