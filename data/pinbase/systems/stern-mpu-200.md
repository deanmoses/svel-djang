[[manufacturer:stern-electronics]]' updated solid-state platform, introduced around 1979, still based on the Motorola 6800 architecture with additional capabilities over the MPU-100. Notable games include [[title:meteor]] (1979), [[title:galaxy-stern]] (1980), and [[title:flight-2000]] (1980). [[manufacturer:stern-electronics]] closed in 1985, unrelated to the later [[manufacturer:stern-pinball]] founded by [[person:gary-stern]] in 1999.
