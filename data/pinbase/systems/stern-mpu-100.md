[[manufacturer:stern-electronics]]' first solid-state pinball MPU, introduced in 1977, based on the Motorola 6800 CPU. It was used on early [[manufacturer:stern-electronics]] solid-state titles including [[title:pinball-stern]] (1977), [[title:stars]] (1978), and [[title:memory-lane]] (1978). The MPU-100 established [[manufacturer:stern-electronics]]'s entry into the solid-state era.
