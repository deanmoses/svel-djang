An evolution of [[manufacturer:stern-pinball]]'s SPIKE platform, introduced around 2017, adding support for a full-color LCD display in the backbox replacing the traditional DMD. The distributed node-bus architecture is retained from SPIKE. Titles include [[title:star-wars-stern]] (2017), [[title:jurassic-park-stern]] (2019), [[title:led-zeppelin]] (2020), [[title:godzilla-stern]] (2021), and [[title:james-bond-007-60th-anniversary]] (2022).
