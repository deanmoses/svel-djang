A further revision of System 11, used from approximately 1988 to 1989, with continued refinements to the sound and control hardware. Notable games include [[title:cyclone]] (1988), [[title:banzai-run]] (1988) with its vertical backbox playfield, and [[title:police-force]] (1989).
