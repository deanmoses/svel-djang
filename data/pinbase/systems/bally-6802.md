A [[manufacturer:bally]]/Midway pinball platform from the early-to-mid 1980s using the Motorola 6802 processor, an evolution of the 6800 with onboard RAM. This transitional platform bridged the gap between [[manufacturer:bally]]'s original solid-state boards and the later 6803 system.
