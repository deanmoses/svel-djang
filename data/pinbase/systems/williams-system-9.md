Introduced in 1984, [[manufacturer:williams]] System 9 used the Motorola 6802 CPU and was a short-lived transitional platform between System 7 and System 11. Only a small number of games were produced, including [[title:space-shuttle]] (1984) and [[title:comet]] (1985). It featured improved sound capabilities.
