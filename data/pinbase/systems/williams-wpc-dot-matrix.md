The second WPC generation, adding a 128x32 dot-matrix display for animations and advanced scoring displays. Introduced in 1991, notable early DMD games include [[title:terminator-2-judgment-day]] (1991) and [[title:the-addams-family]] (1992), the best-selling pinball machine of all time with over 20,000 units produced.
