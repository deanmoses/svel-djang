An updated [[manufacturer:playmatic]] pinball platform, continuing to use the CDP 1802 COSMAC processor. Used on games from the early 1980s including Meg-Aon (1981). The platform evolved the earlier MPU-1 design with added features while retaining the distinctive COSMAC architecture.
