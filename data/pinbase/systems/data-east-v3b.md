A minor revision of the [[manufacturer:data-east]] Version 3 hardware, used in the mid-1990s as the brand transitioned to [[manufacturer:sega]] Pinball following [[manufacturer:sega]]'s acquisition of [[manufacturer:data-east]]'s pinball division in 1994. Games on this platform include [[title:maverick]] (1994) and [[title:batman-forever]] (1995).
