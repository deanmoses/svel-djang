A WPC revision introducing the DCS (Digital Compression System) sound board, which provided CD-quality audio via ADPCM compression. Introduced in 1993, notable games include [[title:indiana-jones-the-pinball-adventure]] (1993), [[title:demolition-man]] (1994), and [[title:the-shadow]] (1994). The DCS audio was a significant leap in pinball sound quality.
