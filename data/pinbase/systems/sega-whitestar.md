[[manufacturer:sega]] Pinball's most advanced and final hardware platform, introduced in 1995, based on a Motorola 6809 CPU with a 128x32 DMD and BSMT2000 sound chip. The platform continued under [[manufacturer:stern-pinball]] after their 1999 acquisition of [[manufacturer:sega]]'s pinball division. Notable [[manufacturer:sega]]-era titles include [[title:star-wars-trilogy]] (1997) and [[title:starship-troopers]] (1997).
