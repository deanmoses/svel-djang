A modified version of the [[manufacturer:sega]] Whitestar platform, continued by [[manufacturer:stern-pinball]] after acquiring [[manufacturer:sega]]'s pinball division in 1999. [[manufacturer:stern-pinball]] made incremental improvements to the Whitestar design over its production run, which lasted until approximately 2005. Notable Stern Whitestar games include [[title:the-simpsons-pinball-party]] (2003), [[title:lord-of-the-rings]] (2003), and [[title:the-sopranos]] (2005).
