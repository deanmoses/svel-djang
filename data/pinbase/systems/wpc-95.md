The final major WPC revision, introduced in 1995, featuring a redesigned and consolidated main board that reduced component count and manufacturing cost. Notable games include [[title:congo]] (1995), [[title:attack-from-mars]] (1995), [[title:medieval-madness]] (1997), [[title:cirqus-voltaire]] (1997), and [[title:monster-bash]] (1998) — several of which are among the most sought-after pinball machines ever made.
