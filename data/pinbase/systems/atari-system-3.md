[[manufacturer:atari]]'s third and final pinball hardware generation, used from approximately 1979 to 1985, built around a 6502-family CPU. Notable titles include [[title:road-runner]] (1979). [[manufacturer:atari]] exited the pinball business in 1985 after declining sales.
