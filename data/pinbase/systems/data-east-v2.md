[[manufacturer:data-east]]'s second-generation platform, introduced around 1988, featuring an upgraded processor while retaining alphanumeric segmented displays. Notable games include [[title:secret-service]] (1988), [[title:torpedo-alley]] (1988), [[title:robocop]] (1989), and [[title:phantom-of-the-opera]] (1990). The platform was succeeded by [[system:data-east-v3]], which introduced dot-matrix displays.
