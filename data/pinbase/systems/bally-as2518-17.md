[[manufacturer:bally]]'s first solid-state pinball MPU, introduced in 1977, based on the Motorola 6800 CPU. Used on early [[manufacturer:bally]] solid-state games such as [[title:freedom]] (1977) and [[title:night-rider]] (1977). This board established [[manufacturer:bally]]'s entry into the solid-state era, replacing their earlier electromechanical designs.
