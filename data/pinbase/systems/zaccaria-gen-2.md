[[manufacturer:zaccaria]]'s second-generation solid-state platform, used from the early 1980s onward, transitioning to a Motorola 6802 CPU. This brought [[manufacturer:zaccaria]]'s hardware more in line with American manufacturers' architectures. Notable games include [[title:pinball-champ]] (1982), [[title:time-machine-zaccaria]] (1983), and [[title:farfalla]] (1983). [[manufacturer:zaccaria]] ceased pinball production in the late 1980s.
