An early [[manufacturer:sega]] Pinball hardware revision following [[manufacturer:sega]]'s 1994 acquisition of [[manufacturer:data-east]]'s pinball division. This transitional board was used in the mid-1990s as [[manufacturer:sega]] rebranded the [[manufacturer:data-east]] pinball line.
