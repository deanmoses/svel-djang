Introduced around 1979, [[manufacturer:williams]] System 6 used the Motorola 6808 CPU and added support for background sound effects and more complex game rules. Notable games include [[title:firepower]] (1980), one of the first multi-ball pinball machines with lane-change features, and [[title:black-knight]] (1980), featuring a two-level playfield with Magna-Save.
