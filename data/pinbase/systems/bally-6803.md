[[manufacturer:bally]]/Midway's mid-1980s pinball platform, based on the Motorola 6803 CPU, used from approximately 1985 to 1989. Notable titles include [[title:cybernaut]] (1985), [[title:escape-from-the-lost-world]] (1987), and [[title:blackwater-100]] (1988). This was the last [[manufacturer:bally]]-designed system before [[manufacturer:williams]] acquired [[manufacturer:bally]]/Midway.
