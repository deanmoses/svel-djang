[[manufacturer:game-plan]]'s second-generation MPU, used from approximately 1981 to 1985. It offered expanded capabilities over the MPU-1 while retaining the 6800-family architecture. Notable games include [[title:attila-the-hun]] (1984) and [[title:cyclopes]] (1985). [[manufacturer:game-plan]] exited the pinball industry in the mid-1980s.
