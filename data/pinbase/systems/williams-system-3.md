[[manufacturer:williams]]' first solid-state pinball platform, introduced in 1977, based on the Motorola 6800-family architecture. It debuted with [[title:hot-tip]] (1977) and [[title:lucky-seven]] (1977). The system used 6-digit numeric displays and established [[manufacturer:williams]]' solid-state design philosophy.
