One of [[manufacturer:playmatic]]'s later hardware revisions, used in the mid-to-late 1980s. It continued the CDP 1802 lineage that defined all [[manufacturer:playmatic]] solid-state platforms. [[manufacturer:playmatic]] eventually ceased pinball production in the late 1980s as the European pinball market contracted.
