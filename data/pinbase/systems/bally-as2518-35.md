An updated version of [[manufacturer:bally]]'s 6800-based MPU, used from approximately 1979 to 1981. This board added support for more complex game rules and additional hardware features over the -17 board. Notable games include [[title:xenon]] (1980), one of the first pinball machines with synthesized speech, and [[title:eight-ball-deluxe]] (1981).
