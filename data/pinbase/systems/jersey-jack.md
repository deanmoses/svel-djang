[[manufacturer:jersey-jack]]'s hardware platform, debuting with [[title:the-wizard-of-oz]] (2013), built around an Intel Celeron PC-class processor running Linux. It features a full-color high-definition LCD display in the backbox, RGB LED lighting throughout, and sophisticated audio. Subsequent titles include [[title:the-hobbit]] (2016), [[title:dialed-in]] (2017), [[title:pirates-of-the-caribbean-jersey-jack-pinball]] (2018), and [[title:guns-n-roses-jersey-jack-pinball]] (2020).
