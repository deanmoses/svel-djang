An incremental update to [[manufacturer:williams]] System 3, introduced in 1978, retaining the 6800-family CPU. Games include [[title:phoenix-williams]] (1978) and [[title:pokerino]] (1978). The update primarily addressed minor hardware revisions and manufacturing improvements.
