A free game awarded to the player for exceeding a score threshold, completing a specific in-game objective, or winning a match at the end of the game. The replay is the coin-operated amusement industry's fundamental reward: play well enough and you earn another game without inserting another coin. Replay thresholds could be set by the operator to control how frequently free games were awarded, balancing player satisfaction against revenue. In jurisdictions where replays were legally classified as gambling, manufacturers offered [[gameplayfeature:add-a-ball]] as an alternative reward system.
