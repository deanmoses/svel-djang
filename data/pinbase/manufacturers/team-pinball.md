Team Pinball is an Australian independent pinball manufacturer, part of the wave of small-batch boutique producers that have emerged globally since the 2010s. Operating from Australia — a country with a passionate pinball culture and a well-established competitive playing scene — Team Pinball builds machines for enthusiasts who value original design and the craftsmanship associated with limited production.
