Riot Pinball is a Spanish independent pinball manufacturer producing machines for the global enthusiast market. The company represents the contemporary continuation of Spain's long involvement in pinball manufacturing — a tradition stretching back to the [[manufacturer:playmatic]] and [[manufacturer:recel]] era of the 1970s — reimagined for the small-batch, direct-to-collector model that has defined the new wave of independent manufacturers.
