MAC S.A. was a Spanish pinball manufacturer active during the late [[technologygeneration:electromechanical]] and early [[technologygeneration:solid-state]] eras. Operating at a time when European manufacturers occupied a distinct market from their American counterparts, MAC produced machines that circulated primarily through Spanish and broader European distribution networks. Their catalog reflects the transitional period of the late 1970s and early 1980s, when the industry was grappling with the shift from relay-driven to microprocessor-based technology, and offers a window into the parallel development of pinball culture on the Iberian Peninsula.
